Top Five Restaurants

Our Top Five Favorites

1. Frothy Monkey

Image Source:frothymonkey.com

Step into the vibrant and laid-back atmosphere of Frothy Monkey, a cafe that exudes hip and chill vibes. Known for its inviting ambiance and cozy seating, this cafe is the perfect spot to unwind and enjoy a delicious meal. Indulge in their signature Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto Pasta, a flavorful and satisfying dish that will tantalize your taste buds. Whether you're looking for a relaxing place to grab a coffee or a tasty meal, Frothy Monkey is the place to be for a hip and delectable experience.

2. Cafe 4

Image Source: ubereats.com

Visit Cafe, a charming establishment that offers a delectable selection of Southern classics paired with an impressive array of wines and beers. The comfortable dining room provides the perfect setting for a relaxing meal, complete with a cozy mezzanine coffee shop for those looking to unwind with a warm beverage. Don't miss out on their signature macaroni, a dish sure to delight your taste buds. Experience the warmth and hospitality of Cafe today!

3. Yassin's Falafel House

Image Source: insideofknoxville.com and visitknoxville.com

Yassin's Falafel House is a beloved family-owned restaurant that prides itself on being welcome to all and serving up fresh, healthy dishes in a fast-casual setting. Specializing in Mediterranean, Greek, and Middle Eastern cuisine, this cozy eatery delights customers with the bold flavors and authentic tastes of the region. From mouthwatering falafel wraps to savory hummus platters, each dish is prepared with love and care, making every bite a journey to the bustling streets of the Mediterranean. Step into Yassin's Falafel House and experience a taste of tradition and warmth that will keep you coming back for more!

4. Tomato Head

Image Source: thetomatohead.com and downtownknoxville.org

Visit this hip American cafe-bakery that serves light, hearty & vegetarian fare including pizza with beer & wine. Try their one-of-a-kind meals when you're craving healthy yet savory bites. Their menu includes pizzas, sandwiches, salads, burgers, and pastas.

5. Hello Tea House

Image Source: utdailybeacon.com and downtownknoxville.org

Enter the calm and cozy vibes of this cafe that attracts people of all ages. Their light and sweet environment is welcoming and a great place to study when you want a bite. Try their variety of boba tea, coffee, cakes, crepe sandwiches, ice creams, and other sweet treats!
